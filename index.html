09FAQ

Common questions.

Is this software or a service?
Both. A managed AI scanner with a dedicated engineer behind it. You aren't logging into a dashboard alone.
What does the trial cost?
Steps 02–06 of How It Works are free for seven days. No card on file. After the week, we agree on a custom monthly retainer — or you walk away with the sheet.
Why not just use ChatGPT or Claude on my own?
Frontier models without guardrails, evals, and brand-voice tuning drift fast. The wrapper around the model is the work. That wrapper is what we build and maintain.
Will my main Instagram account get flagged?
No — it's never used. Scraping happens from a dedicated burner account that we set up with you on day one.
What happens if the model gets a Reel wrong?
Tag it in your sheet's dropdown. The correction goes straight back into the system prompt, and your engineer rolls it into the next weekly tune. The same miss won't repeat.
Where does my data live?
In your own Google Drive + Sheets. We don't host or sell it.
