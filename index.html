07FAQ

Common questions.

Software or service?
Both. A managed AI scanner with a dedicated engineer behind it.
What does the trial cost?
Steps 02–06 are free for 7 days. No card. After that, a monthly retainer — or walk away with the sheet.
Why not just use ChatGPT or Claude on my own?
Without guardrails, evals, and brand-voice tuning, models drift fast. The wrapper around the model is the work.
Will my main IG account get flagged?
No — it's never used. Scraping runs on a dedicated burner.
What if the model gets a Reel wrong?
Tag it in the dropdown. Correction goes back into the prompt. Same miss won't repeat.
Where does my data live?
In your own Google Drive + Sheets. We don't host or sell it.
